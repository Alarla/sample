Company's Brief History

The Crofil Manpower Service Contractor Corporation is a manpower and recruitement agency that is duly recognized by the government of the Repulic of the Philippines. CROFIL started its operation on June 28, 2005, with partnership of Croatian and Filipino nationality registered in Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE). Crofil is a stable company with a main office and a satelite office.

Mission

We will provide the most qualified personnel in the optimum period of time without sacrificing tue quality of the job aspirant. We will provide job aspirants the maximum renumeration packages commensurate to their qualifications.

Vission

We will accomplish the requirement of the prospective employers to have the most qualified personnel through integrity, equal opportunit and competency of every job aspirants. We will uplift the quality of life of job aspirants by providing high-quality employers that will provide enumeration packages equivalent to their skills and capabilities.
