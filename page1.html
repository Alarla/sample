What do the creators say?

The creators of this website are bonafide Grade 12 students of Bulacan State University Senior High School Program both belonging to Accountancy, Business and Management strand. This website serves as a final project for Empowerment Technologies. Aside from accomplishing the project, the creators wish to help fellow students in the same field, businessmen and researchers by making a website which makes those people mentioned be united so entrepreneurship could be developed and improved.

"I'm so glad we're given an opportunity like this to contribute to the society through this website making. Not only we've learned how to create website, but we also developed the values of being concern and thoughtful to our fellow students, researchers and businessmen. I hope we have served the world of entrepreneurship even in this simplest way we could do as a student."

Alyssa M. Domingo
Creator, Understanding Business

"My name is Carla, a 17 year old Filipina Senior High School student. Welcome to the website where you can ask and look for information on how to start a business. If you are planning to make a businss, you can ask a guide or tips to our already successful businessmen/women. You can ask entrepreneurs about their experiences while running their business."

Carla Joy L. Censon
Creator, Understanding Business
